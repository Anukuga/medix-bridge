Welcome to Dr. Elizabeth Blackwell’s Health Hub

I’m Dr. Elizabeth Blackwell, the first woman in the United States to earn a medical degree. Throughout my career, I’ve dedicated myself to making healthcare more compassionate, inclusive, and empowering. This system is designed to carry forward that vision by giving you transparent, secure access to your health records and ensuring that your care is collaborative and informed.

Here, you’re more than just a patient—you’re a partner in your health journey. With easy access to your records and direct communication with your healthcare team, this system is here to support your well-being at every step. Thank you for joining me in the mission to make healthcare a place of empowerment, respect, and care..

Patient-Centered Care Foundation

Unified Medical History
